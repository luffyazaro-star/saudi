About Saudi Marine Offshore

Saudi Marine Offshore is a professional recruitment partner specializing in marine, offshore, engineering, oil & gas, and EPC projects. We support employers and candidates with efficient staffing, compliance management, and fast mobilization across regional and international operations.

Our strength lies in understanding vessel operations, offshore safety, and the technical workforce required for complex energy projects.

Our mission is to connect capable professionals with opportunities that keep offshore and marine operations safe, efficient, and future-ready.
